Atapi's Noises & Numbers Vol. 1


An album containing 100 shortwave radio recordings of noise and spy numbers stations, inspired by The Conet Project.

Including an all star cast such as Oblique, Russian Man, HM01, and The Buzzer! *audience cheering*
